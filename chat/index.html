Watch the work, not a spinner

Every search, edit, and test run shows up as a step as it happens. You follow the agent’s actual moves instead of waiting on a black box and hoping.

Steerable mid-task

It’s a conversation, not a fire-and-forget job. Correct it, redirect it, or answer a question the moment it comes up, before it spends ten more steps going the wrong way.
