Ask it anything. The result lands here.

Ask out loud; it reaches into whatever sources you've given it and answers, in voice. Organise, draft, ask, capture.

Voice-first, hands-free

Talk to it; it talks back. No window to sit in front of, no keyboard. It runs on the main thread and only speaks when it has something worth saying.

Point it at any source

You choose what it can reach: your email, your calendar, a database, your spaces, any MCP server at all. It works with whatever you give it, not a fixed list.

Not just an organiser

Triage is one use. It’s a realtime line to AI for anything you’d normally do at a keyboard: ask a question, draft something, kick off work, capture a thought. Just without the screen.

It’s just an extension

Sources, voice, and actions are all MCP under the hood, so giving it a new source is handing it a tool, not rewriting an app. Point it at anything. It grows as fast as MCP does.
