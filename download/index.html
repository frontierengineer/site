Desktop app

Frontier’s desktop app runs on macOS, Windows and Linux. Open this page on your computer to download — or email yourself a reminder and grab it when you’re back at your desk.

earlyadopters@frontierengineer.com

After you download

  1. 01

    Download & open

    Grab the app for your platform and double-click. It’s a normal, signed desktop app — nothing to install on the command line, no Docker, no script to paste. It works the moment it opens, with voice running locally.

  2. 02

    Connect a host

    From inside the app, attach a host. It uses the SSH access you already have, installs the right software for you, and wires everything together — no terminal.

  3. 03

    Add machines

    Add machines from your SSH config, twenty at a time. The app probes each box, installs the worker daemon, and connects it home. Picking the boxes is your whole job — the app does the rest.

Your code stays on your machines — the host only routes messages and holds metadata. Nothing is uploaded to us.
